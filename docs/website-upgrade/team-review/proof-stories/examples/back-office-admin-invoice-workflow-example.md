## Internal Reference

Internal project nickname: Invoice intake and admin approval workflow

Based on: teams that process invoices, requests, approvals, reminders, reconciliation tasks, and status follow-up manually.

Do not reveal: client name, vendor names, invoice amounts, banking details, account numbers, private approvals, accounting system configuration.

## Safe Public Label

Operations-heavy SMB with back-office admin workload.

## Industry And Business Type

Home services, manufacturing, professional services, education, retail/ecommerce, hospitality, or any admin-heavy SMB.

## Before State

Invoices and admin requests arrived through email, PDFs, scans, forms, and staff messages.

The team manually extracted details, checked missing fields, routed approvals, sent reminders, updated spreadsheets or accounting systems, and tracked exceptions.

## Channels Involved

- Email.
- PDF attachments.
- Forms.
- Shared inbox.
- Spreadsheet.
- Accounting system.
- Approval queue.
- Internal notifications.

## Systems And Tools Involved

Use safe public categories:

- Accounting platform.
- Shared inbox.
- Document storage.
- Spreadsheet or database.
- Approval workflow.
- Notification tool.

Generalize vendor names, invoice amounts, bank details, and account codes.

## What The Automation Did

- Captured invoice or request details.
- Extracted vendor, date, amount, due date, category, and missing fields where safe.
- Checked for duplicates or incomplete data.
- Routed items for approval.
- Sent reminders.
- Created an audit log.
- Prepared data for accounting or spreadsheet updates.
- Escalated exceptions.

## Human Controls

Human-owned:

- Payment approval.
- Vendor changes.
- Account coding exceptions.
- Duplicate conflicts.
- Unusual amounts.
- Sensitive financial decisions.

The AI should not initiate payments or approve expenses without explicit human approval.

## Outcome And Value

Safe outcome language:

- Less manual invoice triage.
- Faster approval routing.
- Fewer missed reminders.
- Better exception visibility.
- Clearer audit trail.

Metrics to collect:

- Items processed.
- Manual data entry reduced.
- Approval cycle time.
- Duplicate/exception flags.
- Reminder completion.
- Admin hours saved.

## Buyer Fear This Helps Address

- Financial control: payments and approvals stay human-owned.
- Accuracy: exceptions are flagged.
- Spend anxiety: starts with intake and routing before deeper accounting automation.
- Auditability: creates logs and review trails.
