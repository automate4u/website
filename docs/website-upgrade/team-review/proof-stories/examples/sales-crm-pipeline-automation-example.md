## Safe Public Label

B2B service business or sales-led SMB.

## Industry And Business Type

B2B services, home services, manufacturing, professional services, technology, or any sales-led SMB.

## Before State

Leads arrived through calls, forms, email, chat, and referrals. Staff manually qualified each lead, copied details into CRM, remembered follow-up tasks, and decided which opportunities needed immediate attention.

Hot leads could sit too long. CRM records were incomplete. Follow-ups depended on individual discipline instead of a consistent operating workflow.

## What The Automation Did

- Captured lead source, contact details, intent, urgency, budget range, and service interest.
- Scored or categorized the lead using approved rules.
- Created or updated CRM records.
- Created follow-up tasks.
- Routed high-intent leads to staff.
- Prepared a summary for the salesperson.
- Sent an approved confirmation or next-step message.
- Logged source page, CTA, UTM context, and workflow interest.

## Human Controls

Human-owned:

- Qualification rules.
- Pricing.
- Final proposal.
- Sales judgment.
- High-value account handling.
- Sensitive or strategic opportunities.

The AI should not promise pricing, availability, discounts, or deal terms unless approved.

## Outcome And Value

Safe outcome language:

- Faster lead response.
- Cleaner CRM records.
- Fewer forgotten follow-ups.
- Better visibility into which pages and offers generate interest.
- Less manual data entry for sales/admin staff.

Metrics to collect:

- Lead response time.
- CRM completion rate.
- Follow-up tasks created.
- Meetings booked.
- Missed follow-ups reduced.
- Source-to-conversation conversion.
