## What The Automation Did

Managed AI Operations is not one automation. It is the operating layer around deployed automations.

Typical work:

- Monitor conversations, workflow completions, escalations, and failures.
- Review transcripts, summaries, and edge cases.
- Tune prompts, rules, and routing.
- Track usage, cost, and value indicators.
- Update workflows when business rules change.
- Review sensitive cases.
- Maintain integration health.
- Report monthly outcomes and next improvements.

## Human Controls

Human-owned:

- Business policy changes.
- Approval thresholds.
- Sensitive customer handling.
- Escalation standards.
- Budget decisions.
- Final decisions about expanding automation.

The AI system should not silently change behavior without review.

## Outcome And Value

Safe outcome language:

- Production AI stays monitored instead of abandoned after launch.
- Buyers understand who owns reliability, tuning, and improvement.
- Workflow issues are easier to detect and correct.
- Usage and cost are reviewed against business value.
