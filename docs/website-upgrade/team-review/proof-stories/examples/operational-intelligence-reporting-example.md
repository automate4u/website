## Safe Public Label

Owner-led business with recurring operational workflows.

## Industry And Business Type

Any SMB or mid-market operator managing calls, support, quotes, bookings, orders, admin requests, or multi-location operations.

## Before State

Leadership did not have a clear view of what work was happening, where requests were delayed, which workflows escalated, or whether automation was creating measurable value.

Reports were manual, irregular, or spread across multiple tools.

## What The Automation Did

- Collected workflow events from connected systems.
- Summarized handled requests, escalations, failures, response time, bookings, and follow-ups.
- Highlighted exceptions.
- Sent weekly or monthly summaries.
- Created alerts when thresholds were crossed.
- Gave staff and leadership a shared view of operational performance.

## Human Controls

Human-owned:

- KPI definitions.
- Thresholds.
- Business interpretation.
- Staff performance decisions.
- Strategic changes.

The AI should not make management decisions from metrics without context.

## Outcome And Value

Safe outcome language:

- Better visibility into operating work.
- Easier measurement of automation value.
- Faster identification of bottlenecks.
- Clearer monthly improvement conversations.

Metrics to collect:

- Requests handled.
- Escalation rate.
- Response time.
- Workflow completion rate.
- Failed sync/action rate.
- Bookings or follow-ups completed.
- Manual hours avoided.

## Buyer Fear This Helps Address

- Spend anxiety: shows whether automation is creating value.
- Maintenance anxiety: makes issues visible.
- Trust: separates claims from measured workflow data.
- Adoption: gives leadership and staff a shared operating picture.
