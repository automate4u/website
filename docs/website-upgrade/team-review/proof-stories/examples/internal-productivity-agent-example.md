## What The Automation Did

- Summarized meetings.
- Extracted tasks and owners.
- Drafted SOP updates.
- Answered internal questions from approved documents.
- Prepared weekly project updates.
- Routed blockers or overdue items.
- Suggested follow-up messages.

## Human Controls

Human-owned:

- Final task ownership.
- Sensitive HR or financial decisions.
- Strategy decisions.
- SOP approval.
- Customer commitments.
- Internal communications that require judgment.

The AI should not make personnel, financial, or strategic decisions.
