## Safe Public Label

Marketplace, directory, or inventory-heavy business.

## Industry And Business Type

Retail/ecommerce, local marketplace, professional directory, wellness platform, manufacturing parts, property/HOA knowledge, or service marketplace.

## Before State

Users struggled to find the right product, vendor, service, document, or record because search was too literal, filters were incomplete, location mattered, availability changed, or the data was inconsistent.

Staff had to answer questions manually when the discovery experience failed.

## Channels Involved

- Website search.
- Marketplace/directory pages.
- Internal admin search.
- Catalog/inventory database.
- Location data.
- Availability or status fields.
- Customer support questions.

## What The Automation Did

- Indexed searchable records.
- Added filters and facets.
- Supported location or availability-based discovery.
- Tuned relevance.
- Improved admin tooling for catalog quality.
- Used search behavior to identify missing data, bad records, or support gaps.
- Connected search failures to support or content improvements.

## Human Controls

Human-owned:

- Ranking priorities.
- Featured/promoted listings.
- Sensitive location handling.
- Catalog quality decisions.
- Business rules.
- Public claims.

The system should not hide important records or promote items without approved business rules.
