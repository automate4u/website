## What The Automation Did

- Centralized requests into a review queue.
- Summarized context from multiple systems.
- Suggested next actions.
- Showed confidence, missing information, and escalation flags.
- Let staff approve, edit, reject, or route actions.
- Logged decision history.
- Updated downstream systems after approval.
- Reported volume, cycle time, escalations, and completion status.
