## Internal Reference

Internal project nickname: Missed-call recovery and dispatch intake

Based on: home services, HVAC, plumbing, electrical, repair, or field-service workflows.

Do not reveal: company name, service area, technician names, customer addresses, pricing, emergency policies, dispatch rules.

## Safe Public Label

Regional home services company.

## Industry And Business Type

Home services / HVAC / field services. Mostly B2C. SMB.

## Before State

Calls arrived when office staff were already on another call, after hours, or while technicians needed dispatch support.

Some callers left voicemails or contacted competitors before receiving a callback.

Staff manually collected job details, location, urgency, preferred appointment times, equipment notes, and customer contact details.

## Channels Involved

- Phone calls.
- Email follow-up.
- Contact forms.
- SMS confirmations.
- CRM.
- Calendar or booking tool.
- Dispatch or field-service software.
- Service policies and FAQs.

## Systems And Tools Involved

Use safe public categories:

- Phone/voice agent.
- CRM.
- Calendar or booking tool.
- Dispatch tool.
- SMS/email notifications.
- Quote follow-up queue.

Generalize dispatch software, pricing models, service territory, and customer details.

## What The Automation Did

- Answered missed or after-hours calls.
- Captured service type, location, urgency, contact details, and preferred timing.
- Classified emergency, quote, repair, maintenance, scheduling, or follow-up request.
- Created booking path, dispatch task, or callback task.
- Sent customer confirmation or next-step message.
- Notified office staff or assigned team.
- Escalated emergency, pricing, warranty, or unusual situations.

## Human Controls

Human-owned:

- Emergency priority.
- Technician assignment.
- Pricing.
- Warranty decisions.
- Final booking conflicts.
- Unusual service requests.

The AI should not guarantee arrival time, price, warranty approval, or emergency service unless approved rules allow it.

## Outcome And Value

Safe outcome language:

- Fewer missed opportunities.
- Faster callback readiness.
- Cleaner service intake.
- Less front-office interruption.
- Better quote follow-up tracking.

Metrics to collect:

- Missed calls recovered.
- Appointments booked.
- Quote follow-ups completed.
- Average time to callback.
- Office admin time saved.
- Jobs created from after-hours calls.

## Buyer Fear This Helps Address

- Spend anxiety: ties value to recovered calls and booked work.
- Customer experience risk: callers get immediate acknowledgement.
- AI mistakes: urgent or sensitive situations route to staff.
- Integration complexity: starts with task/booking creation.
