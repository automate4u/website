## What The Automation Did

- Captured request details.
- Classified risk level.
- Prepared a recommended response or action.
- Showed missing information.
- Routed to the right reviewer.
- Let staff approve, edit, reject, or escalate.
- Logged the decision and context.
- Updated downstream systems after approval.

## Human Controls

Human-owned:

- Final approval.
- Sensitive decisions.
- Policy interpretation.
- Legal/medical/financial/custody-related actions.
- Customer commitments.
- Exception handling.

The AI should not bypass the review queue for high-risk actions.
