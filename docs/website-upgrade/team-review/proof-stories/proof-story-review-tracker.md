# Proof Story Review Tracker

Use this file to track which proof stories are rough notes, approved examples, or ready for the live website.

## Status Labels

- **Idea:** possible story, not captured yet.
- **Captured:** rough notes exist in `filled-submissions/`.
- **Drafted:** Codex has created public-facing copy.
- **Needs review:** team needs to check accuracy, tone, NDA safety, and claims.
- **Approved for representative use:** can be used as a representative workflow pattern.
- **Approved as sanitized case study:** can be used as a real but anonymous/sanitized case study.
- **Internal only:** useful for sales calls, not public website.
- **Blocked:** missing permission, metric, asset, or legal review.

## Review Checklist For Each Story

- The public label is broad enough to protect the client.
- The workflow sounds specific and real.
- The copy does not claim a metric we cannot defend.
- Human control is clear.
- Sensitive data and identifying details are removed.
- The story maps to a real buyer fear.
- The story has a clear website placement.
- The story has at least one useful asset idea: diagram, mock screenshot, transcript, event log, dashboard, or before/after map.
