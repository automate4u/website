How integration works

Our integration approach follows a layered architecture designed for reliability and maintainability:

  • Lead capture & qualification: Inbound inquiries from web forms, calls, and texts are processed through intelligent qualification engines that assess buyer intent and route to appropriate agents.
  • Property data sync: Automated pipelines pull listing data from MLS and property management systems, enriching it with market insights and making it instantly available for client inquiries.
  • Transaction orchestration: A central workflow engine coordinates document generation, milestone tracking, and stakeholder notifications throughout the buying, selling, or leasing process.
  • Client relationship management: Bi-directional sync ensures contact records, showing history, and communication preferences remain consistent across CRM, calendar, and communication platforms.
